That's me

Simon Kraus

Hello there! My name is Simon and I am completely into cool designs and visualizations. Mixing them up with geodata into maps or 3D-models is what I'm curious about. Recently I successfully completed my bachelor's degree in Geovisualization at the Technical University Würzburg/Schweinfurt - THWS - based in Würzburg, Southern Germany. In my bachelor thesis I developed a VR scene in Unreal Engine referring to the topic "VR eye tracking - possibilities of data acquisition and interaction in 3D geovisualizations."

In the following you can see some of my projects I created during my studies and spare time. This portfolio website is also a project of mine to further learn the programming languages html, css and java script.
